I'm Ben Strawbridge, a web builder for artists and bands. I work one-on-one to create sites that are fast, personal, and permanent.

The big platforms are built for volume — thousands of sites on shared templates, with your content locked inside their system. WordPress, Squarespace, GoDaddy — I do the opposite. Every site I build is custom, yours to keep, and free from monthly platform fees — whether I manage it for you or hand it off to you entirely.

I'm also a rescue service. If your host shut down, your CMS is outdated, or you're paying too much for a site you don't love — I can fix that.
