Formed in 1998 as a passion project to create an online gallery for friends and family, and as a way to learn how to code websites.  You can still see what it looked like way back in 2002 on the [wayback machine on archive.org](https://web.archive.org/web/20020220112908/http://www.arts-link.com:80/), unfortunately other versions have been lost to posterity.

It went through several design iterations, and has included some efforts at e-commerce through affiliate marketing and recommendations.
